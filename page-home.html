What can computer science do for the world?

This is a project made by first year students of Durham University.

In this project we aim to highlight the good that advances in technology bring to the world and improve our collaboration skills in the process.

Explore

Wildlife

Explore the effect computer science is having on the natural world, including narwhals, saving the bees, and counting elephants!

Health

Discover how technology can tackle hearing, mental health, and breast cancer.

Environment

Read about what computer science is doing to prevent forest fires, find alternative fuels and plant trees.

History

Explore how AI is interpreting rtefacts, languages, and even cave art.
